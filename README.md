# Healthcare Diabetes Risk Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes diabetes patient data and builds a rule-based risk scoring system to classify patients into Low, Medium, and High risk groups.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Methodology
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Feature-based risk scoring
- Patient risk classification

## Risk Rules
- Glucose > 150 → +2
- BMI > 30 → +1
- Age > 60 → +1

## Insights
- Most patients fall into Low Risk category
- A smaller portion are Medium and High Risk
- High glucose levels strongly correlate with increased risk
